How It Works

1. Mint Orders

Projects are submitted as "Work Orders," and WYT tokens are minted against their projected future revenue.

2. Back with Capital

Users buy the newly minted WYT with pUSD, providing the capital that funds the work orders.

3. Earn Yield

As work orders are completed, they pay back revenue to the contract, increasing the pUSD balance and the value of all WYT.
