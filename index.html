How It Works

1. Mint Orders

Projects are submitted as "Work Orders," and WYT tokens are minted against their projected future revenue.

2. Back with Capital

Users buy the newly minted WYT with pUSD, providing the capital that funds the work orders.

3. Earn Yield

As work orders are completed, they pay back revenue to the contract, increasing the pUSD balance and the value of all WYT.

Unlock More Value with the Lending Vault

Borrow Against Your Assets

Already hold WYT? Deposit your tokens as collateral and borrow pUSD against their value without having to sell. Get instant liquidity while your original investment continues to appreciate.

Fund the Vault

Protocol revenue, including redemption fees from the main platform and interest from borrowers, is used to fund the lending vault, creating a self-sustaining financial ecosystem.
